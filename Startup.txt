-- Start hosting the bot:
1. Hold Left Shift and Right Click in the Source Folder
2. Click Open Powershell
3. Run the below commands in order:
node deploy-commands.js
node index.js

-- Turn the bot off, one of the following methods:
1. Hold Ctrl and press C, then close the Powershell
2. Directly close the Powershell (this will take longer for the bot to turn offline)
